superstopper

save crespo.world/timer to your phone or computer's home screen, plug in via usb-c, and you have a physical stopwatch button with only ~8ms of input delay.

there's a small pause on the very first press — the button is waiting to see if you want a different mode. after that it's instant: one tap to start, one to pause, one to reset. full controls in the repo.

no drivers. no software. your computer just sees it as a keyboard.

  • powered by usb-c
  • led indicator (5v or 3.3v selectable)
  • fully remappable

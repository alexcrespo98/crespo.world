pillbutton setup

it comes ready to go. just connect and configure.

first time
  1. plug in the button. it will broadcast a wi-fi hotspot called pillbuttonsetup.
  2. on your phone, go to wi-fi settings and connect to pillbuttonsetup. no password.
  3. come back here, fill in your alarm times, and hit save.
  4. the button restarts and is ready. no internet needed after this.
  5. to reconfigure: hold the button for 5 seconds to re-enter setup mode.

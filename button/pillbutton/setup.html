pillbutton setup

connect your phone to the button's wi-fi, then fill this out.

first time? start here
  1. flash the firmware onto your ESP32-C6 — see the repo for the sketch.
  2. power it on. it will broadcast a wi-fi network called pillbutton-setup.
  3. on your phone, go to wi-fi settings and connect to pillbutton-setup. no password.
  4. come back here and fill out the form below.
  5. next time: hold the button for 5 seconds to re-enter setup mode.

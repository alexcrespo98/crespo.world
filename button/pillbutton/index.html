pillbutton

press it when you take your pills. the led tracks how long it's been and shifts color as time passes, so you can tell at a glance whether you're good, getting close, or overdue.

  • taken recently
  • coming up soon
  • overdue

the transition is gradual — green fades through yellow to red over however long your dose interval is. you set the interval in setup.

save crespo.world/button/pillbutton/setup to your home screen to get back to setup easily.

party emergency

a button. press it and your sonos speakers immediately drop whatever they're playing and start your pre-loaded song or playlist — then return to your normal queue when it's done.

great for happy birthday at a restaurant. you can even cut the song off short early. no phone needed once it's set up.

it just needs to be on the same wi-fi as your sonos speakers. setup takes about a minute.

set it up →

tip: save crespo.world/button/esp32c6/setup to your phone's home screen so it's always one tap away.

  • powered by usb-c (wall or computer)
  • led indicator (5v or 3.3v selectable)
  • works with any sonos system

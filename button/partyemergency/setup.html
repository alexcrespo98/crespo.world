partyemergency setup

it comes ready to go. just connect and configure.

first time
  1. plug in the button. it will broadcast a wi-fi hotspot called partyemergencysetup.
  2. on your phone, go to wi-fi settings and connect to partyemergencysetup. no password.
  3. come back here, fill in the form, and hit save.
  4. the button restarts and joins your home wi-fi. setup is done.
  5. to reconfigure: hold the button for 5 seconds to re-enter setup mode.

partyemergency

press the button and your sonos speakers switch to a pre-selected song or playlist, then return to whatever was playing when it's done. it just needs to be on the same wi-fi as your speakers.

you can set a duration or let the whole thing play out. works with any sonos system.

set it up →

save crespo.world/button/partyemergency/setup to your home screen to get back to the setup page easily.

  • modify friendly
